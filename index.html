PugChamp

(n.) 1. a draft-organized pickup game system. 2. an elite player of competitive mix games.

About

Draft-organized pickup games, in which captains select players for their team from a pool of available players, are a familiar and old concept to many involved in games. PugChamp brings this proven system to the present day with modern feel and functionality.
