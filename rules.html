Rules

Violations of any of the following rules may result in applicable restrictions. Admins are given discretion in enforcing these rules as they see fit for the benefit of the site and its community.

Courtesy

Be respectful to your fellow players.
Do not attempt to impersonate other users.
Do not spam or advertise in chat.

Gameplay

Play to the best of your ability.
Do not ready up if you will not be immediately available to play a full-length game.
Do not add up to captain if you are not able to pick a competitive team or if you are not comfortable playing any of the roles.
Do not add up to a role if you are not comfortable playing that role.
Do not play if you are not able to communicate with your team via Mumble voice.
Do not excessively play roles that you were not drafted to play.

Pro Expectations

By being a member of the Pro League, a player is recognized as one of the best and a potential role model, and is thus expected to live up to the highest standards of courtesy and integrity in their interactions with the PugChamp systems and community.

Players who are unable to follow these expectations will receive appropriate consequences, including possible suspension or expulsion from the Pro League.
