Rules

to ensure our security and continuing stability

Rules

Violations of any of the following rules may result in applicable restrictions. Admins are given discretion in enforcing these rules as they see fit for the benefit of the site and its community.

Courtesy

Be respectful to your fellow players.
Do not attempt to impersonate other users.
Do not play under an alternate account.
Do not spam or advertise in chat.

Drafting

Do not ready up if you will not be immediately available to play a full-length game.
Do not add up to captain if you are not able to pick a competitive team or if you are not comfortable playing any of the roles.
Do not add up to a role if you are not comfortable playing that role.
Do not attempt to play if you are not able to communicate with your team via Mumble voice.

Gameplay

Play to the best of your ability.
Promptly arrive in the game server and Mumble voice server once you have been confirmed into a game.
Play for the full length of the game and do not leave early.
Do not excessively play roles that you were not drafted to play.
Do not attempt to gain unfair advantages.

Pro Expectations

By being a member of the Pro League, a player is recognized as one of the best and a potential role model, and is thus expected to live up to the highest standards of courtesy and integrity in their interactions with the PugChamp systems and community.

Players who are unable to follow these expectations will receive appropriate consequences, including possible suspension or expulsion from the Pro League.

Cheating

Cheating (defined as attempting to obtain and utilize an unfair advantage against other players) is a serious violation of competitive integrity and cannot be tolerated.

As such, any player suspected of cheating will be immediately and indefinitely suspended. A team will then review all available evidence (including any submitted by the player) to determine the player's guilt. If cheating is determined to be probable, the player will be permanently banned.

In addition, for the protection of the service, new accounts deemed to be suspicious may be suspended until further investigation.

FAQ

Q: Under what circumstances can I override someone to play in a role they did not add as?
A: It depends on the situation. In general, if there is a role with no players left except for overrides, the captain should take on that role. However, in certain cases an override may be desirable in order to make the game better. (Note that medic overrides are disabled and a captain must play medic if none are left to pick.) Any captain who attempts to abuse the override system may be restricted.

Q: Under what circumstances am I allowed to play other roles?
A: Generally, you should play the role you were drafted to play. The one recognized exception is offclassing (from scout or roamer) in order to attack or defend the final control point. Players who are deemd to be excessively offclassing may be restricted.

Q: Under what circumstances can I let a draft expire as captain?
A: Captains should not allow drafts to expire with very rare exceptions. A captain intentially allowing a draft to expire may be restricted.
