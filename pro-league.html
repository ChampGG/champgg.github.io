PugChamp Pro League

a stage for legends and challengers alike

About

The PugChamp Pro League is a system exclusively available to top players that have proved themselves hardy legends as well as upcoming players that have proved themselves worthy challengers.

Contestants

Only the best will find themselves with an invite, and they must be careful not to lose it.

Legends

Players who have been tested by playing at the highest levels of league competition in their continent will be recognized and automatically invited.

Challengers

Players who have proved themselves worthy for trial by being at the top level of open competition each month will be promoted to the Pro League.

Fallen

Each month, any player whose Pro League rating is too low will be relegated back to open competition and must requalify for the Pro League.
